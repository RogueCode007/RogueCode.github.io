We are developing digital solutions to help people beat chronic disease

The challenge

1 in 3 people in Nigeria have a chronic disease or is at risk of one. In Africa, more than 200 million people are affected. LifeBox is developing clinically validated digital programs using mobile technology, evidence-based medicine and behavioral science to help people prevent and manage chronic disease

Introducing Sustain

Sustain, by LifeBox, is a scalable, digital health solution for prevention and management of type 2 diabetes, and hypertension. The program helps people change the habits that put them at risk and provides the education, resources, and support they need to change their habits and achieve their health goals
